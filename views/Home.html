In the Cloud

Your books, ratings, notes, bookmarks, and all other library data are all stored in the cloud allowing you to continue a book easily on one device that you started on another.

Library Management

Xyfir Books is built with simple library management available from any device in mind. Updating a book's metadata like its title, authors, rating, or even cover is a piece of cake. You can even edit multiple books at once. Most all popular ebook formats can be uploaded and edited.

Ebook Reader

Xyfir Books is more than just library management and storage, it's also an ebook reader. xyBooks comes with all the features you would expect from an ebook reader like bookmarks, notes and highlighting, and more. All with the benefits of instantly having all your data synced to the cloud so you can easily pick up where you left off on another device. Our reader is available on any device you can access Xyfir Books with and can read EPUB format ebooks.

Xyfir Annotations

Xyfir Annotations allows you to better explore and interact with your ebooks. When reading a book you can search for and download annotations for your book. Once downloaded, annotations will be available to use while reading. Enabling annotations mode while reading allows you to click on highlighted items in your book and view that item's annotations. An annotation can be a document, a wiki link, an image, and many other things.

All new xyBooks users are given a free one-month subscription to xyAnnotations when they sign-up.
