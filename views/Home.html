In the Cloud

Your books, ratings, notes, bookmarks, and all other library data are all stored in the cloud allowing you to continue a book easily on one device that you started on another. No longer do you have to deal with manually transferring libraries.

Library Management

Libyq is built with simple library management available from any device in mind. Updating a book's metadata like title, authors, rating, or even cover is a piece of cake. Want to edit multiple books a once? You can do that too!

Ebook Reader

Libyq is more than just library management and storage, it's also an ebook reader. Libyq comes with all the features you would expect from an ebook reader like bookmarks, notes and highlighting, and more. All with the benefits of instantly having all your data synced to the cloud so you can easily pick up where you left off on another device. Our reader is available on any device you can access Libyq with and can read ePub format ebooks.

Libyq Annotations Support

Libyq Annotations allows you to better explore and interact with your ebooks. When reading a book you can search for and download annotations for your book. Once downloaded, annotations will be available to use while reading. Enabling annotations mode while reading allows you to click on highlighted items in your book and view that item's annotations. An annotation can be a document, a wiki link, an image, and many other things.

Calibre Compatible

Calibre is a widely popular suite of tools for ebook reading and library management. However, it limits you to a desktop environment and doesn't enable you to easily access and modify your library from other devices. Since Calibre is such a popular program and still very useful for advanced library management we have made Libyq entirely compatible with Calibre libraries. You can upload your Calibre library to Libyq and if you ever need to, re-download the updated and still-Calibre-compatible library.
